# Loan-Approval-Analysis-Risk-Profiling-Using-EDA-and-Predictive-Insights
This dataset contains applicant details such as demographics, income, loan amount, credit history, and property area. It is used to analyze factors influencing loan approval decisions and to build models that predict whether a loan application will be approved or rejected.

## 📖 Overview

In this project, I analyzed loan applicant data containing demographic details, income, loan attributes, and credit history.

The goal was to explore patterns in the data and identify factors that impact loan approval decisions, helping simulate real-world banking risk assessment.

---

## 💼 Business Problem

Financial institutions receive thousands of loan applications, making it difficult to assess applicant risk efficiently.

Key challenges include:

* Identifying reliable applicants
* Understanding factors influencing loan approval
* Managing risk based on credit history and income
* Ensuring fair and consistent decision-making

This project aims to analyze applicant data and uncover patterns that help improve loan approval decisions.

## 📂 Dataset

* Source: Kaggle
* Dataset: Loan Prediction Dataset

### Features Included:

* Applicant demographics (Gender, Married, Dependents, Education)
* Financial details (Applicant & Coapplicant Income, Loan Amount)
* Loan attributes (Loan Term, Property Area)
* Credit history
* Loan status (Approved / Rejected)

## 🔍 Research Questions

### 👥 Demographic Analysis

* What is the percentage of men and women applying for loans?
* What proportion of applicants are married vs unmarried?
* What percentage are graduates vs non-graduates?
* What is the proportion of self-employed vs salaried individuals?

### 💰 Income Analysis

* What is the average applicant income?
* How is applicant income distributed?
* How is coapplicant income distributed?

### 📊 Credit & Dependents Analysis

* How many applicants have good credit history?
* How are applicants distributed across dependents categories?

### 🔗 Relationship Analysis

* What is the relationship between gender and loan approval?
* Does education level influence loan approval?
* How does self-employment impact approval rates?
* How strongly does credit history affect loan approval?

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Credit history has a **strong impact** on loan approval decisions
* Applicants with higher income show better approval chances
* Semiurban areas tend to have higher approval rates
* Dependents and financial burden influence approval probability
* Education and employment type have moderate impact
